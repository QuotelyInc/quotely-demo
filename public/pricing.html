Pricing Questions & Answers

Are there any setup fees or hidden costs?
No setup fees, no implementation costs, no hidden charges. What you see is what you pay. Unlike Applied Rater ($2,000+ setup) and EZLynx ($1,500+ implementation), we include everything in your monthly subscription.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, you can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. No long-term contracts required, though annual plans receive a 10% discount.
What's included in the free trial?
Full access to all Professional plan features for 14 days, including GAIL AI competitive intelligence, all integrations, and unlimited support. No credit card required.
How does pricing scale as we add users?
Simple per-user pricing. Add or remove users monthly. Volume discounts available for 10+ users. Enterprise plans offer custom pricing for large agencies.
Is API access really free?
Yes, full REST API access is included at no extra cost. Unlike Applied Rater ($100-200/month) and EZLynx ($50-150/month), we don't charge extra for integrations.
What if I need help migrating from my current system?
We provide free migration assistance for all new customers. Our team will help export your data and get you set up quickly - usually within 24-48 hours.
